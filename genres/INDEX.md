## Genre Combinations

These genres commonly blend well together or have natural crossover potential:

### Rock + Electronic
- [Synthwave](synthwave/README.md) + [Rock](rock/README.md) = Synth-rock, darksynth
- [Industrial](industrial/README.md) + [Metal](metal/README.md) = Industrial metal
- [New Wave](new-wave/README.md) + [Post-Punk](post-punk/README.md) = Post-punk revival
- [Shoegaze](shoegaze/README.md) + [Electronic](electronic/README.md) = Ethereal wave
- [Noise Rock](noise-rock/README.md) + [Industrial](industrial/README.md) = Industrial noise rock (Swans, Killing Joke)

### Punk Fusions
- [Pop Punk](pop-punk/README.md) + [Emo](emo/README.md) = Pop-emo, pop-punk revival (Fall Out Boy, My Chemical Romance)
- [Pop Punk](pop-punk/README.md) + [Ska Punk](ska-punk/README.md) = Ska-pop-punk (Reel Big Fish, Less Than Jake)
- [Crust Punk](crust-punk/README.md) + [Black Metal](black-metal/README.md) = Blackened crust (Iskra, Martyrdod)
- [Crust Punk](crust-punk/README.md) + [Sludge Metal](sludge-metal/README.md) = Sludge crust (Dystopia, His Hero Is Gone)
- [Crust Punk](crust-punk/README.md) + [Post-Rock](post-rock/README.md) = Neocrust (Tragedy, Fall of Efrafa)
- [D-Beat](d-beat/README.md) + [Crust Punk](crust-punk/README.md) = D-beat crust (Wolfbrigade, Skitsystem)
- [D-Beat](d-beat/README.md) + [Thrash Metal](thrash-metal/README.md) = D-beat thrash (Disfear, Tragedy)
- [Screamo](screamo/README.md) + [Post-Rock](post-rock/README.md) = Post-screamo, skramz (Touche Amore, La Dispute)
